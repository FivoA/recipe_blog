Simple Tomato Pasta

20 min
Vegetarian
Serves 2
Quick: one pan sauce + boiled pasta.
Ingredients
  • 200 g pasta
  • 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Steps
  1. Boil pasta according to package directions; reserve 60 ml cooking water.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic ~30s until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, simmer 6–8 min. Add salt & pepper.
  4. Toss pasta with sauce; add reserved water if needed to loosen.
  5. Serve immediately.

Pan-Seared Salmon

15 min
Pescetarian
Serves 2
Crispy skin, tender inside.
Ingredients
  • 2 salmon fillets (approx. 140 g each)
  • Salt, pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon
Steps
  1. Pat salmon dry, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high. Place salmon skin-side down; press 20s to keep flat.
  3. Cook 4–5 min skin-side, flip and add butter; cook 1–2 min more.
  4. Squeeze lemon and serve.

Overnight Oats

Prep 5 min
Vegan option
Serves 1
Prep the night before.
Ingredients
  • 60 g rolled oats
  • 200 ml milk or plant milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional)
  • Sweetener to taste
Steps
  1. Combine oats, milk, chia, and sweetener in a jar.
  2. Stir, seal, refrigerate overnight (6–8 hrs).
  3. Top with fruit and eat cold.
