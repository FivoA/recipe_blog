habanero hot sauce

10 min
fills 250ml bottle
grow your own habaneros to be extra cool
ingredients
  • 2 medium sized habanero peppers
  • 60ml white wine vinegar
  • 60ml water
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 medium sized red bell pepper
  • 1 carrot
  • juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
steps
  1. wash, peel and rough chop veggies (use gloves for habanero, be careful!)
  2. blend it all up until smooth and fill into bottle
  3. if too thick, add more water. if too watery, add some more carrot/bell pepper
  4. keep in fridge for up to 2 weeks

chewy chocolate chip cookies

90 min
yields ~8 cookies
chewy and thick with lightly crispy exterior
ingredients
  • 115g butter
  • 125g brown sugar
  • 110g sugar
  • 185g ap flour
  • 170g 70% chocolate
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • splash of vanilla extract
steps
  1. melt and brown (stir constantly and be patient) the butter over medium low heat
  2. whisk in white and brown sugar till well combined
  3. whisk in vanilla extract and egg until smooth and a bit creamy
  4. sift in flour, baking powder and fold in until just combined
  5. rough chop chocolate and gently fold in
  6. form small balls out of dough (~8) and refrigerate for at least 45min
  7. heat oven to 175c (top and bottom heat) and bake for ~15min until they just turn golden brown
  8. let cool for 5min, then finish cooling on a wire rack
